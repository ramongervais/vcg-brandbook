Sales Guidelines

Consultative, not pushy — we guide traders to explore, we don’t pressure them

Key Principles

  1. Lead with curiosity, not features
  2. Ask about their market interests, not their budget
  3. Frame trading as a skill to develop
  4. Introduce VCG ONE as a co-pilot, not a magic tool
  5. Never promise profits or guaranteed outcomes

Approved Phrases

“What markets are you most curious about?”
“Let me show you how traders are watching this move”
“VCG ONE can help you understand your trading patterns”
“Trading is a skill — the platform helps you develop it”

Avoid

“You’ll make money with this”
“This is a guaranteed opportunity”
“You’re missing out if you don’t trade now”
“Just deposit and start making profits”
Do

Listen first, then recommend. Share market context. Celebrate their curiosity. Frame the platform as a tool for growth.

Don’t

Push for deposits. Promise returns. Use urgency or FOMO tactics. Compare to gambling or get-rich-quick schemes.
